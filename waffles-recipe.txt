Ingredients:
- Milk/water
- Instant pancake/waffles mix (Just add milk/water)
- Some butter or oil
- Honey or maple syrup

Directions:
1. In a bowl, add the product
2. Add the water/milk
3. Mix until it looks uniform, it is possible to modify the proportion in order to get a more liquid/thick mix
4. Pre-heat the waffle machine
5. Butter on all the surface
6. Add the mix until the waffler is full
7. Wait until the waffles are gold and crunchy
8. Enjoy with honey or maple syrup over them
